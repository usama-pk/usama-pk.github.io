I am a PhD student at Information Technology University (ITU), Pakistan. I have just submitted my PhD thesis. My thesis is supervised by Prof. Dr. Junaid Qadir. My thesis addresses the problem of adversarial machine learning (ML) attacks on networking applications and provides procedures for desiging defensive interventions against these attacks.

Previously, I completed my MS degree from National Univeristy of Computer and Emerging Sciences (NUCES), Pakistan. My thesis was supervised by Prof. Dr. Sheikh Sajid.

*I am actively looking for post-doctoral opportunites.
